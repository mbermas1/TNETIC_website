About TNETIC

We build software that earns its keep.

Founded in Chicago, TNETIC, Inc. is a software company focused on one thing: creating tools that reduce friction, eliminate manual work, and help businesses operate with confidence.

Our mission

Make powerful software accessible to every business — not just the enterprise.

We believe that small and mid-sized businesses deserve the same quality of software as Fortune 500 companies. Every product we build is designed to be genuinely useful, approachable without an IT department, and built to scale with you.

What we stand for

Values we build by

01

Usefulness over features

We ship tools that solve real problems, not feature lists that look good in demos but create complexity in practice.

02

Accessible complexity

Powerful under the hood, simple at the surface. Our products don't require dedicated IT staff to deploy or operate.

03

Honest partnership

We tell you what your project actually needs — not what fills an invoice. Long-term relationships matter more than one-time wins.

04

Designed to scale

Every product we build is architected to grow. You shouldn't need to rebuild when your business doubles.

05

Responsive support

When something breaks or a question comes up, you reach a person. Not a chatbot. Not a ticket queue measured in days.

06

Chicago-grounded

We're a Chicago company. We work in Central Time, we value directness, and we show up when it matters.

i-Attend

i-Attend replaces sign-in sheets, manual rosters, and disconnected spreadsheets with a single, cloud-based platform that handles every step of the attendance lifecycle — from registration through certificate delivery.

Built for organizations where attendance carries real weight: continuing professional development programs that must document CEUs, schools and districts tracking student participation, Greek-letter organizations managing chapter meeting records, houses of worship logging service attendance, and conference organizers who need auditable, compliance-ready data.

If your event requires a verifiable record of who was there — i-Attend was built for it.
